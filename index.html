Reasoning, in the open

Every answer shows its work.

The transaction lifecycle — Pending → Relayed → Mempool → Confirming → Confirmed — is the agent's live reasoning timeline. Each tool it runs prints a coloured stage pill; you can stop, inspect, or override at any step. Nothing touches your keys without confirmation.

Pending
thinking
Parses your intent and plans which sources to touch.
Relayed
running · querying
Walks outputs and hops across the UTXO graph.
Mempool
reading
Checks pending transactions and live fee pressure.
Confirming
drafting
Composes the answer or a transaction for your review.
Confirmed
done
Returns the result — nothing signed without confirmation.
